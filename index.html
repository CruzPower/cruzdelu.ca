About Me

I’m Cruz. I’m a tech enthusiast who’s been surrounded by gadgets and systems since I can remember. Right now, I’m balancing school, my job at Food Basics, and trying to push myself to learn something new every day—whether that’s diving deeper into AI or figuring out how to make better videos.

I’ve always had a thing for video production—whether it’s editing footage for fun, working on small projects, or just figuring out the technical side of things like lighting and sound. The cool part about this tech world is how quickly it evolves, and I’m always chasing after that next thing, that next big project that challenges me.

Beyond just the software and equipment, I really enjoy the creative process. It’s one thing to know how to use tools, but it’s another to make something that feels real, something that connects with people. That’s why I’m drawn to video—there’s a story behind every frame, and it’s exciting to be able to piece that together.

When it comes to being a tech rep, I see it as more than just fixing problems. It’s about bringing ideas to life. Whether it’s helping people get their equipment set up for a project or working on something bigger, I want to make sure everyone has the tools they need to get the job done. Because at the end of the day, it’s all about creating and making things work.
